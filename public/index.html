Modular is a cutting-edge framework for creating highly customizable Discord bots

Functionality

Visual scripting

Open source

Modular is one of the most feature-rich general purpose Discord bots out there. You can create, update and delete messages, categories, channels, permissions, emoji, reactions, roles and commands. We offer modules for embeds, buttons, and much more.
